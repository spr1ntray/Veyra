## STYLE NOTES

The town square is the primary gameplay location where the player trains. The minimalist design:
- **Empty center**: Provides space for the training dummy and player actions without visual clutter
- **Silent atmosphere**: Few buildings, mostly shadows — creates isolation and focus
- **Single warm accent**: Orange lamp glow provides the only non-dark colour, grounding the player in a specific time/place
- **Pixel gradients**: Subtle shading within each block suggests form without photorealism

The square feels like an abandoned place, reinforcing the dark fantasy tone. The player is alone with their magic.
