## STYLE NOTES

The mage's home is the inventory/equipment screen location. The minimalist interior design:
- **Tall empty space**: Conveys the tower's height, isolation, and scale
- **Single purple accent**: Neon purple runes are magical, mystical, and dark — perfect for a mage's study
- **Minimal furniture**: Just enough to suggest this is a home, not overwhelming the player's view
- **Harsh shadow**: Single light source creates drama, mystery, and atmosphere
- **Clean geometry**: No messy details — every shape has purpose

The room feels like a place of power and knowledge. It's where the player manages their gear before facing the world.
