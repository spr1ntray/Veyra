## STYLE NOTES

The world map is the navigation screen. The minimalist approach:
- **Abstract terrain**: Terrain is suggested through colour, not detail
- **Neon location markers**: Each zone has one colour, making it easy to distinguish and navigate
- **Vast empty space**: Much of the map is dark, reinforcing the sense of a large, largely unexplored world
- **No unnecessary information**: No grid, no compass, no labels — just pure exploration potential

The map feels like a strategic overview of a dark world the player must conquer. The neon markers are the only guides in a vast darkness.
