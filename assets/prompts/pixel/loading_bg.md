## STYLE NOTES

This loading screen acts as the game's first visual impression. The minimalist approach (30% content, 70% empty space) creates grandeur through absence rather than detail. The single neon cyan accent draws the eye to the portal/castle without requiring complex visual effects.

Key decisions:
- **Minimalism**: Vast empty space conveys scale and isolation
- **Colour focus**: Neon cyan is the only bright colour, creating magical intrigue
- **Pixel style**: 16-32bit era (not crude 8-bit, but not modern) maintains retro aesthetic while feeling polished
- **Atmospheric mist**: Dark purple/charcoal tones tie the composition together without adding detail clutter
- **High contrast**: Sharp silhouette vs. dark background creates drama
