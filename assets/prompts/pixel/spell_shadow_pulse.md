## STYLE NOTES

Shadow Pulse is an area-of-effect attack spell dealing dark magical damage. The icon design reflects this:
- **Green colour**: Visually distinct from cyan (arcane) and purple (buff), this colour signals "attack" or "damage"
- **Concentric rings**: Suggest expanding waves of energy hitting everything
- **Radial symmetry**: Perfect balance suggests omnidirectional impact
- **Sharp geometry**: Crisp lines convey fast, sharp damage
- **Radiating outward**: Motion is outward and explosive, not directed like the bolt

The icon is instantly recognizable as an area spell, visually communicating its mechanics through shape alone.
