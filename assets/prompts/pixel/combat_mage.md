## STYLE NOTES

The combat mage is the player's avatar. The sprite design prioritizes:
- **Dynamic pose**: Action posture shows capability and readiness
- **Minimal detail**: Focus on silhouette and colour, not texture
- **Neon accent**: Single cyan glow shows magical capability and ties to the magic system
- **Clean pixel forms**: Each pixel contributes to readability and aesthetic
- **Harsh lighting**: Creates drama and reinforces the sense of power being channeled

The sprite should read instantly as "mage casting spell" even at game size (small screen position). Colour and silhouette are the design language.
