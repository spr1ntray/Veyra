A spell icon for Arcane Bolt, minimal pixel art style, 16-32bit inspired, dark fantasy game asset.

Icon concept: A simple geometric representation of a magical projectile. The primary image is a sharp pointed shape (arrow, bolt, or diamond) in neon cyan (#00d9ff) pointing diagonally upward-right, suggesting motion and direction. The bolt has hard, sharp pixel edges, not rounded or soft.

Symmetry: Slightly asymmetrical (bolt points diagonally right, not perfectly centered) to suggest direction and action. Not perfectly balanced — it's a projectile in motion, not a resting object.

Readability: Must be readable at 64×64 size. Every pixel counts. The silhouette of the bolt is clear and distinct even if squinted at.

Render: Pure pixel art, no anti-aliasing, no blur, no soft edges. Sharp geometric forms in bright cyan against black background. Game asset quality.

## STYLE NOTES

Arcane Bolt is the first spell the player learns — a basic ranged magical attack. The icon design reflects this fundamental role:
- **Cyan colour**: Instantly identifies this as "arcane" magic in the game's established colour language
- **Bolt/arrow shape**: Clear visual metaphor for projectile attack and direction
- **Directional energy**: Pointing diagonally shows action and forward momentum
- **Minimal design**: Only what's necessary to communicate spell purpose — no extra flourish
- **High contrast**: Bright cyan on black makes it pop on all UI backgrounds
- **Sharp edges**: Reinforces the idea of a sharp, clean magical projectile

The icon is distinctive and readable at 64×64, the game's standard icon size. Visually distinct from Focus (circular/balanced) and Shadow Pulse (if used).
