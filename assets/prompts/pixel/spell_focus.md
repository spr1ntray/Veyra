## STYLE NOTES

Focus is a buff/utility spell that enhances concentration and magical power. The icon design reflects this protective, stabilizing role:
- **Purple colour**: Identifies this as "protective" or "buff" magic in the game's colour system (distinct from cyan arcane)
- **Circle geometry**: Symbolizes wholeness, balance, inward focus, and protection (shield-like)
- **Center point**: Shows the focal point of concentration and the "self" at center
- **Cardinal dots**: Suggest balanced magical energy radiating from the center at all sides
- **Centered composition**: Perfect balance implies stability, grounding, and protection
- **Sharp geometry**: Pure geometric shapes communicate clarity and mental focus

The icon is visually distinctive and balanced, visually opposite to the directional Arcane Bolt. Clear visual distinction makes gameplay intuitive.
