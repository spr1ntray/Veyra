# Veyra Dark Fantasy RPG — Style Guide

## Visual Style
- **Rendering Style**: Pixel art (retro-inspired, 16-bit aesthetic)
- **Art Direction**: Dark fantasy, minimalist, atmospheric, magical
- **Mood**: Mysterious, brooding, mystical, melancholic
- **Era/Setting**: Medieval fantasy with magical elements, arcane architecture
- **Reference Keywords**: pixel art, retro RPG, dark fantasy, minimalist, atmospheric, arcane

## Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**:
  - Deep Navy Blue: `#1a2d4d` (dominant, main background tone)
  - Dark Violet/Purple: `#2d1a3d` (secondary, mystical accent)

- **Accent Colors**:
  - Gold/Amber: `#d4af37` (magical highlights, rune accents)
  - Pale Silver: `#b8c5d6` (secondary highlights, moonlight)

- **Secondary Palette**:
  - Deep Indigo: `#0f1b33`
  - Dusty Plum: `#4a2c5e`
  - Twilight Purple: `#3d2a47`

- **Tone Rules**: Desaturated, muted, cool-toned. Avoid bright colors, neon, or oversaturation. Gold sparingly for magic/special elements.

## Typography Aesthetics
- **Font Mood**: Serif or ornate for titles (medieval fantasy feel), sans-serif for UI
- **Weight**: Medium to bold for headings, regular for body text
- **Visual Language**: Elegant but austere, readable despite darkness of background

## Lighting & Atmosphere
- **Direction**: Soft overhead/side lighting or no clear light source (mystical ambience)
- **Quality**: Dim, shadowy, diffused moonlight or arcane glow
- **Mood**: Low-key, mysterious, slightly eerie, magical luminescence
- **Special Effects**: Subtle glow from magical elements, fog/mist, shadows

## Forbidden Elements
- Bright colors (neon, candy pastels, oversaturated hues)
- Modern elements (technology, contemporary architecture, signs)
- Overly detailed photorealism
- Comic/cartoon style (should be pixel art, not illustration)
- Cheerful or cute aesthetics (must remain dark and moody)
- Skulls/gore (dark but not grimdark)
- Any non-medieval architecture or vehicles

## Per Asset Type Rules

### Loading Screen Backgrounds (9:16 vertical)
- Must have clear dark area in center/lower third for text overlay
- Atmospheric depth through layering (foreground, mid-ground, distant background)
- Single focal point or balanced composition
- Tonal range: mostly dark with strategic light accents
- No legible text, logos, or watermarks
- Specific style: pixel art, 512×768 or 768×1024 resolution
- Suggested themes: castle/tower, magical portal, forest ruins, starry sky, ancient monument

### Character Portraits (1:1 square)
- Head-to-shoulders minimum, full-body preferred
- Clear expression and detail on face
- Center-focused composition
- Background dark and non-distracting (blur or muted color)
- Color-accurate magical attire/auras

### Icons (512×512, scalable)
- Clear silhouette readable at small sizes
- Flat pixel art (no gradients)
- Centered composition with padding
- High contrast against dark backgrounds
- Gold accents for important/magical items

### UI Elements (Frames, panels, buttons)
- Minimalist design
- Ornate corner details in gold or silver only
- Dark backgrounds with subtle texture
- Clear functional areas
- No busy patterns, high readability

## Reference Keywords
Use these anchors in ALL prompts:
- pixel art
- dark fantasy
- minimalist
- mysterious atmosphere
- arcane/magical
- medieval setting
- retro RPG aesthetic
- desaturated palette
- cool tones (blue/purple/silver/gold)
